Help & Support

Find answers to common questions and troubleshoot issues.

Offline FAQ

  • **Why is there a "Sync Pending" message?** This means attendance data is saved on this device and waiting to be sent to the cloud. It will sync automatically when a network connection is found.
  • **My camera is not working.** Please check that the device has camera permissions enabled and that the camera is not being used by another application.
  • **What if a student is not recognized?** Use the manual entry or a manual override feature (not shown in this prototype) or ask the student to re-align their face within the frame.
  • **How do I update a student's record?** Navigate to the "Student Records" page, find the student, and click "Edit."
